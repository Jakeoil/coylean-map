Pipe Junction

Reference geometry for the line-polygon model of encroachment: a junior (red, vertical) line meeting a senior (blue, horizontal) line, projected as two cylinders sharing a common top plane.

For equal diameters the taper is a 45° chevron whose tip sits at the intersection. For unequal diameters the junior runs flat for a moment, then converges. This page is a static reference for future expansion of the Encroach overlay.
