Turtle Paradise

The elaborate render, brought to life — each glyph a small creature that breathes, sways, and blinks. The first entry replays the hand sketch from turtle paradise.md, keyed by the lone F to the V77 glyph in assignments.json. The rest are the lettered glyphs themselves — the real engine, dressed in the same green / yellow / pink — growing in as seedlings. Turtles all the way down.

Entry one is the baked sketch (no runtime decompression — the shapes live in turtle-paradise-data.js). The seedlings come from computeGlyphMatrices in glyphs/glyph-core.js, the same propagation the catalog and map use. Add a row to CATALOG in turtle-paradise.mjs to grow the paradise.
