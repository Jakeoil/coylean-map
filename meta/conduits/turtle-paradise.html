Turtle Paradise

The elaborate render, brought to life — each glyph a small creature that breathes, sways, and blinks. The selected glyph lives large above; the collection of twelve sits below. Pick any one to bring it up into the hero. F starts selected — keyed by the lone F in the hand sketch (turtle paradise.md) to the V77 glyph in assignments.json. The other eleven are the real engine, dressed in the same green / yellow / pink. Turtles all the way down.

The collection · twelve glyphs

Entry one is the baked sketch (no runtime decompression — the shapes live in turtle-paradise-data.js). The seedlings come from computeGlyphMatrices in glyphs/glyph-core.js, the same propagation the catalog and map use. Add a row to CATALOG in turtle-paradise.mjs to grow the paradise.
