Scene I · The Conduits

The Yellow Conduit

We are down at a scale where the ground is turtle-shell on turtle-shell, turtles all the way down — white and green tile under tile, too fine to name. The first thing big enough to read is the yellow conduit. It is our limit: the world is rendered just coarsely enough that a single symbol or number fits inside each conduit segment, and no finer. What runs below it we have not reached yet.
