Descent

The Coylean map in its elaborate dress — every cell a nest of rectangles, color-coded by depth. Drag to pan, scroll or pinch to zoom, and turn the depth knob to split the glyphs into deeper generations — another shell of color surfaces from within. Turtles all the way down.

The map is the real engine — built with coylean-core.js’s Propagation.fromUniverseBoundary (a boundary-seeded universe, not a hand-rolled seed) — drawn in the draw map’s elaborate nested-rectangle style (the 19-color depth palette). Zoom and pan are free navigation; the depth knob raises depth, surfacing the inner nested layers.
