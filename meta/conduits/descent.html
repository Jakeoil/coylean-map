The Conduits · a living descent

Descent

The Coylean map in its elaborate dress — every cell a nest of rectangles, color-coded by depth. It breathes on its own; touch it to descend a generation. Each step splits the glyphs into their children and another shell of color surfaces from within. Turtles all the way down.

The map is the real engine — built with coylean-core.js’s Propagation.fromUniverseBoundary (a boundary-seeded universe, not a hand-rolled seed) — drawn in the draw map’s elaborate nested-rectangle style (the 19-color depth palette). Descending raises depth, surfacing inner layers; the camera eases inward so the children grow into view. Click the map to dive.
