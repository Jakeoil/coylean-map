The Coylean Square

The ruler made into a map. This is a proper Coylean square — one cell of north/west context reseeded through Propagation.fromUniverseBoundary, so it is the genuine SE patch of an infinite map, not a boundary-clipped grid. Each line's thickness is its priority, so the ruler's skeleton is visible: switch the ruler and watch it move from powers of two to Fibonacci numbers. The trailing result lines are suppressed.
