Ruler grid on a horn torus

Horn torus: the minor circumference 2πr equals the major circumference 2πR, so r = R and the inner hole degenerates to a single point. The two ruler families — tube cross-sections and major circles — share the same arc length, so dyadic divisions on each are directly comparable. Line thickness follows the 2-adic ruler pattern: 0, 1, 2, 1, 3, 1, 2, 1, 4…
