Tromino Explorer

A tromino is not a bigger glyph — it is the 3-cell context key (self, North, West) that decides how one glyph refines into its 2×2 children. At the Home Anchor the cage wall seals and self alone decides. At any other anchor the wall leaks, so the same self splits differently depending on its North and West neighbours — that is why you need the L-tromino. Click any cell in the map to load its tromino; edit the slots from the catalog.
