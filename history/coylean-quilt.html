The Coylean Quilt

♥    ❦    ♥

Stitched by hand, by my wife, and given to me as a complete surprise — for (we think) my 50th birthday. She took the Coylean map I have chased for so long and made it warm: every patch a piece of the pattern, every seam a small act of love.

It was a wonderful surprise, and it is one of my most treasured things. A map you can wrap around yourself.

♥ ❤ ♥ ❤ ♥
Thank you, my love.

For her — with all my heart.
