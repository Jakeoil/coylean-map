Minimal encoding

A Coylean map of any order is fully determined by:

1. Two ∞-positions (where evenness(0) sits on each diagonal family)
2. Storage order implies seniority — first = senior (/), second = junior (\)
3. The evenness priority ordering is universal (not stored)
4. / vs \ is implicit from cell position (m+n parity)

The two ∞-diagonals initialize to true. Everything else follows from XOR propagation.
